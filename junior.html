Junior Coaching - Ages 9-13

Our Junior coaching is designed for children aged 9 to 13, focusing on creating a fun, engaging, and supportive environment where every child feels encouraged to strive for their best.

Our goal is to foster a love for athletics while building foundational skills that will support their development both on and off the track.

Program Highlights:
  • Fun and Engagement - Making athletics a positive and enjoyable experience
  • Skill Building: Developing proper techniques, speed, agility, and coordination.
  • Consistency for Growth: Encouraging regular participation for long-term development.
  • Pathway to Success: Supporting young athletes aiming for competitive improvement.

Our Approach

We believe in nurturing young talent through a balance of structured training and fun. By creating an environment that is supportive and engaging, we aim to instill discipline, teamwork, and a love for physical activity in young athletes, preparing them for long-term success in their sports journey.
