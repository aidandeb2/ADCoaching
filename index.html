About Me

My name is Aidan Debernardi and I am a World Class athletics coach with over 10 years of experience, specialising in sprint training, biomechanics, and athletic development. My passion is helping athletes at all levels achieve their personal best through individualised coaching.

Coaching Qualifications:
  • Australia Athletics Level 3
  • World Athletics Level 1

My Coaching Philosophy

My coaching philosophy is built on the belief that every athlete has unique potential, and with the right support and guidance, they can achieve their best. I emphasise developing strong fundamentals, improving biomechanics to enhance efficiency, and fostering a safe and enjoyable training environment. By focusing on consistency and building confidence through achievable goals, I aim to inspire athletes to grow and thrive, whether they're striving for personal progress or competitive success.
